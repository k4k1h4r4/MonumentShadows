Eighteen Tests of Will and Craft

Monument Shadows presents a course that demands patience, precision, and a willingness to wrestle with the High Plains wind. Each hole carries a name earned through character — holes that whisper, and some that shout.

From the opening challenge of Omen to the unforgiving final reckoning on 18, the course rewards the bold and humbles the careless.
